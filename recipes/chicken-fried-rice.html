Chicken Fried Rice

Description

A timeless favorite that brings comfort with every bite. Our Chicken Fried Rice is made with tender, juicy chicken, fluffy jasmine rice, and crisp vegetables, all stir-fried in a savory blend of soy sauce, garlic, and sesame oil. It’s simple, satisfying, and packed with flavor—perfect as a main dish or a hearty side.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked jasmine rice
  • 1 cup diced chicken breast
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (peas, carrots, bell peppers)
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons sesame oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Green onions, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Heat sesame oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat.
  2. Add garlic and sauté until fragrant.
  3. Add diced chicken and cook until browned and cooked through.
  4. Push the chicken to one side of the skillet and pour in the beaten eggs. Scramble until fully cooked.
  5. Add mixed vegetables and stir-fry for 2-3 minutes.
  6. Add cooked rice, soy sauce, salt, and pepper. Stir-fry until everything is well combined and heated through.
  7. Garnish with chopped green onions before serving.
