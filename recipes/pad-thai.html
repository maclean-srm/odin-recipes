Pad Thai

Description

A beloved Thai classic bursting with sweet, savory, and tangy flavors. Our Pad Thai features rice noodles stir-fried with tender chicken, crisp vegetables, and scrambled eggs, all tossed in a tamarind-based sauce and topped with crunchy peanuts and fresh lime. It’s a vibrant, satisfying dish that brings restaurant-quality taste to your kitchen.

Ingredients

  • 8 oz rice noodles
  • 1 cup sliced chicken breast
  • 1 cup bean sprouts
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 3 tablespoons Pad Thai sauce
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Chopped peanuts and lime wedges (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Soak rice noodles in warm water until softened, then drain and set aside.
  2. Heat vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat.
  3. Add garlic and sauté until fragrant.
  4. Add sliced chicken and cook until browned and cooked through.
  5. Push the chicken to one side of the skillet and pour in the beaten eggs. Scramble until fully cooked.
  6. Add softened noodles, Pad Thai sauce, salt, and pepper. Stir-fry until everything is well coated and heated through.
  7. Garnish with bean sprouts, chopped peanuts, and lime wedges before serving.
